## Our Project in a Nutshell

Welcome to Scralgebra, a game that links Scrabble and mathematics ! 
In this game, you will not form words, but mathematical equalities with the pieces you are given. 
You will be able to use a variety of math symbols like the usual operators, but also the factorial  !  !
You will also be able to customize your games in a lot of ways in the options menu.

This is my first game, don't hesitate to give feedback !
https://matthjass.itch.io/scralgebra


## How to Build our Project

To play this first version of Scralgebra, you first have to download java 21 on your computer :
https://www.oracle.com/ch-fr/java/technologies/downloads/#jdk21-windows
Then, you can download and extract the zip file.
Then, click on the 'scralgebra.jar' and the game should launch !
Make sure the 'src' folder is right next to it.
